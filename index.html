About Me

Computer Science & Engineering student at The Ohio State University, graduating with a bachelors degree in December 2015.

Out of class, I dabble with web and mobile. I love to follow the Rails, Node, and web design communities. As the assistance iOS director, I help lead the newly founded OSU Mobile App Club. We teach fellow students the power behind iOS and Android development. So let's talk about some cool front end, Node.js, Rails, or iOS.

In 2013, I interned at Etactics, a clearinghouse in Stow, Ohio that manages millions of healthcare insurance claims across the globe. I was responsible for turning content from the databases into client readable figures, charts, and reports. A fellow intern and I developed a usage monitoring system, alerting managment when clients showed reduced activity to the system.

In the summer of 2014, I will be interning at J.P. Morgan Chase.
