About Me

Computer Science & Engineering student at The Ohio State University, graduating with a bachelors degree in December 2015.

I dabble with web and mobile. The Rails, iOS, and web design communities are amazing. As the assistant iOS director, I help lead the newly founded OSU Mobile App Club. We teach fellow students the power behind iOS and Android development. So let's talk about some cool front end, Node.js, Rails, or iOS.

As I can't sit in front of a computer all day, I also enjoy running, working out, music, guitar, cooking, basketball, talking, dancing, you name it -- I try to maintain an interest in as many things as possible. Even if you hate computers, we'll get along.

In 2013, I interned at Etactics, a clearinghouse in Stow, Ohio that manages millions of health care insurance claims across the globe. I was responsible for turning content from the databases into client readable figures, charts, and reports. A fellow intern and I developed a usage monitoring system, alerting management when clients showed reduced activity to the system.

I'm currently the Student Influencer at Ohio State for Spotify; the largest music streaming service. We work on integrating and promoting Spotify into college life.

In the summer of 2014, I will be interning at J.P. Morgan Chase.
